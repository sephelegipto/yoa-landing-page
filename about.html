About Me

How did I Start the Offshore Accountant

I’m Jeryl, a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) from the Philippines, who established the Offshore Accountant with a heartfelt mission to empower small accounting and bookkeeping firms in the US and Canada to thrive without the burden of high costs.

A Personal Journey

My journey in establishing this firm began from a place of understanding and empathy. I’ve seen firsthand the struggles that small firms face - tight budgets, limited resources, and the overwhelming pressure to deliver top-notch services while keeping costs low. I knew there had to be a better way to support these businesses by offering both expertise and affordability.

Inspired by a Vision for Better Service

Your Offshore Accountant is determined to assist you make a real difference. I am passionate to create a space where small firms could access expert bookkeeping services without the heavy price tag. Working from home, I saw an opportunity to offer the same high-quality service at a much lower cost, thanks to reduced overhead and a flexible approach.

What Drives Me

I’m driven by a commitment to make professional bookkeeping accessible and affordable for everyone. I believe that small firms shouldn’t have to choose between quality and cost - they deserve both. That’s why I’ve embraced remote work, using cloud-based tools like QuickBooks Online to offer seamless, efficient bookkeeping services globally.

Why Choose Me?

A Partner Who Understands Your Needs

I understand the challenges of managing financial tasks while running a business. My goal is to relieve you of this burden by providing accurate and reliable bookkeeping tailored to your firm’s unique needs.

Smart Solutions That Save You Money

With my “Smart Books, Smart Savings” approach, I focus on delivering value through cost-effective bookkeeping strategies. By working remotely, I keep costs low and pass those savings on to you.

A Personal Touch with Every Interaction

You’re not just another client to me. I genuinely care about your success and I intend to work closely with you to understand your goals, challenges, and aspirations. Together, we can build a stronger financial foundation for your business.

Building a Brighter Future Together

Starting Your Offshore Accountant wasn’t just about creating a business - it was about building a community. I want to be more than just your bookkeeper; I want to be your partner in success. I’m here to help your firm not only survive but thrive. Let’s work together to create a future where your books are managed efficiently, your costs are minimized, and your success is maximized.

Reach out today and let’s start this journey together!
